Harsh Truths

Perfect hair isn't always healthy

One of the biggest misconceptions is that your hair always has to look perfectly styled or “done” at all times. Unfortunately, the hairstyles that are best for growing your hair are not always the ones that look the best or most put-together.

Twists or other hairstyles that don’t require much manipulation can look “basic” or even “childish” at times, but they are often the best hairstyles for growing your hair. At the end of the day, it is the healthy hairstyles that cause your hair to grow, not the ones that look the best.

Extension braids do more harm that good

Braids and extensions are very popular, and when done, they can look very neat. However, it is essential to note that constantly wearing such heavy hair can put a lot of tension on your scalp. Eventually, this can weaken your hair, resulting in hair loss. This is one of the reasons why some people find it difficult to retain hair length. The main focus is on how it looks, rather than how it is affecting your hair. It is essential to take breaks from such styles, as this can help.

Consistency over everything

There is no shortcut for healthy hair. Growing your hair and keeping it long requires being consistent with what you do, like the frequency of washing your hair, keeping it moisturized, and being gentle with hairstyles.

It is not about getting everything right sometimes, but about getting everything right consistently. It is about what you do daily, no matter how simple, which makes the greatest impact on your hair.

Being consistent is beneficial because it helps strengthen your hair, retain moisture, and reduce breakage, which are all factors towards healthy hair and long hair.
