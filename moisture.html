Moisture

Why Moisture?

Moisture means the availability of water in your hair. Since your type 4 hair’s natural structure is coily and has twisted strands, natural oils from your scalp can’t easily travel to your strands. This can lead to dryness, causing your hair to be brittle and weak. This breakage has a direct connection to length retention, as unmoisturized and dry hair tends to break and makes your hair unable to maintain its length. This is why moisture isn’t an option for type 4 hair; it is a must to maintain healthy and strong hair.

Moisture doesn’t just come in a snap; it is an ongoing process that needs consistency, and building your own moisture routine makes keeping up with it easier. These routines can be on wash days, treatments throughout the week (oiling and spraying), or when taking your style down. This ensures your hair stays manageable, soft, and less likely to break throughout the week. By consistently caring for your hair, you can support its health and help it retain length to achieve better results in the long run.

Building your personal moisture routine

While a moisture routine can look similar across people, having your own routine personalized to your hair and preferences is important, because although type 4 hair can be similar, we all have unique hair types and characteristics. What works for me might not work the same for you, so understanding your hair allows you to choose the right products and routines that fit your hair’s needs perfectly. Not only for hair health, but also a routine that fits your schedule is important because it helps you manage your time well and balance your hair and busy schedules, like schoolwork.

Deep Conditioning and Hair Masks

Deep conditioning can help you restore moisture and strengthen your hair. After shampooing your hair, your hair becomes more vulnerable, by deep conditioning your hair at that time, you’re able to restore moisture you lost while washing your hair, while also strengthening your lengths. Consistently doing this can help improve your hair's overall health and make it more manageable. Consistency is key to every routine and supports your length retention journey.

As seen in my wash day videos, using the treatments mentioned can help you consistently care for your hair and provide moisture and support length retention simply and practically. Avocado and flaxseed treatments can deeply moisturize your hair and give it strength. By doing them consistently, you can benefit from their advantages and see real changes in your hair in real time. These masks can help you reduce dryness in your scalp and length and contribute to overall hair health. Through time, if you keep deing hair masks and deep conditioning your hair, these actions will support length retention, as healthier hair is less prone to breakage.
